Pickleball Info

Pickleball is the fastes growing sport whose popularity is spreading not only across the states, but across the world. To help people understand a little more about this wonderful sport, here is a list of why the sport is becoming so popular.

  1. Accessable to everyone
    • Courts are usually open to the public for all to use
    • With pickleball becoming a worldwide sport, people all across the globe can play this sport
  2. Active sport that keeps people healthy
    • Studies have shown pickleball to be a healthy way to not only engage your body in rigorous physical activity but also keeps the mind young
  3. Easy transition from tennis
    • These two sports have a lot in common and any tennis player looking for a new sport will find that they will pick up pickleball increadibly fast due to similar rules and mechanics
  4. Fun for all age groups
    • The sport is extreemly popular for all age groups including those higher in the years
